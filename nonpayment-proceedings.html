Nonpayment Proceedings

Nonpayment proceedings are brought to recover rent arrears and seek possession. These matters can implicate payment records, defenses, and negotiation options.

Key documents

Rent ledger/payment history, notices and demands, lease terms, and communications between the parties.

Potential outcomes

Resolution can include payment plans, stipulations, judgments (including money judgments), or trial depending on the facts.

Our approach

We develop the record quickly, evaluate defenses and leverage, and pursue a path aligned with the client’s business and timing priorities.
