Holdover Proceedings

Holdover cases typically involve possession disputes after lease expiration or termination, and can include claims based on breach, nuisance, or other statutory/contractual grounds.

Common issues

Lease interpretation, notice requirements, service, defenses, and the scope of relief (including use and occupancy).

Strategy

We evaluate procedural posture and evidence early, then pursue motion practice where appropriate and explore settlement options that align with client goals.

Related disputes

Some holdovers lead to broader litigation—such as claims for damages, declaratory relief, or contract enforcement.
