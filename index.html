How the Process Typically Works

Every case is different, but most landlord/tenant matters follow a predictable arc.

  1. Intake & document review: Lease, notices, rent ledger, communications, court papers.
  2. Strategy: Identify procedural defenses, proof gaps, and settlement leverage.
  3. Appearance & motions: Address jurisdiction/service issues, discovery, dispositive motions where appropriate.
  4. Negotiation or hearing/trial: Pursue the best outcome efficiently—settlement, stipulation, or trial-ready litigation.

Tip: Save and bring all notices, petitions, and affidavits of service. These documents frequently determine the outcome.
