Commercial evictions and lease disputes in Queens.

Commercial eviction litigation can impact business continuity, goodwill, and substantial lease obligations. Strategic planning is essential from the first notice of default.

Common disputes

Alleged lease defaults, rent/Additional Rent disputes, compliance issues, assignment/sublet issues, and possession-related litigation.

Documentation matters

Commercial leases often include notice-and-cure provisions, default clauses, and attorney’s fee clauses—details that drive strategy.

Goals

Preserve leverage, protect operations where possible, and pursue efficient resolution—whether by cure, negotiated surrender, or litigation.
